Meet Pawma: your pet's whole story, in one place.

Vets hand you paper. Labs hand you numbers. Pawma turns all of it into a single, owner-held record, so you walk into every visit knowing exactly where your pet stands.

One record

Visits, vaccines, labs & reminders, together.

Every vaccination, every recommendation, every meal and symptom in one timeline. Pawma quietly tracks what's due next, so the rabies booster and the ALT recheck never slip.

✨ Where the AI comes in

Snap a lab report. It reads itself.

Photograph a blood panel or a vet document and Pawma's AI structures it into the record, analyte by analyte, with the normal ranges. Nothing lives in a drawer or a lost line of chat.

Vet-ready

A clear summary for every visit.

One tap turns the record into a summary your vet can read in seconds: history, trends, and what's changed since last time. Less "I think it was a few months ago," more actual care.

Why we're building this.

Pets are family, but their health lives in scattered paper, group chats, and memory. When it matters most, no one can find it.

The Wag Lab Co is a Bangkok-based pet-care company on a simple bet: give every pet a health record its owner truly holds, and better care follows for the pet, the vet, and everyone building to help them.

We started with our own, Cotton and Ester, and the pile of vet visits and lab reports that came with loving them. Pawma is the record we wished we'd had. We're building the rest in the open, with the people who care for pets every day.
